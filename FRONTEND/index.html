Classes

Cardio

Cardio exercises, like running and cycling, help improve heart health, boost stamina, and burn calories, making them essential for overall fitness and endurance.

Power Lifting

Powerlifting is a strength-based sport focusing on three main lifts: squat, bench press, and deadlift. It emphasizes maximal strength development through heavy weightlifting and proper technique.

Personal Training

Personal training is a customized fitness coaching service where a certified trainer designs and guides individualized workout plans. It focuses on achieving specific fitness goals, improving technique, and providing motivation.

Cross Fit

CrossFit is a high-intensity fitness program combining strength training, cardio, and functional movements like lifting, jumping, and sprinting, designed to improve overall endurance and strength.

Yoga

Yoga is a mind-body practice combining physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation. It enhances flexibility, strength, balance, and mental clarity while promoting relaxation and stress relief.

Weight Training

Weight training is a form of exercise using resistance, typically with free weights or machines, to build muscle strength, endurance, and tone. It targets specific muscle groups and enhances overall physical fitness.
